Your BSC wallet address serves as your account. No registration required - just connect your wallet or create a new one. You have complete ownership of your identity, secured by your private key.

Red packets let you share crypto with friends in chats. Create a red packet with BNB or tokens, send it to a chat, and recipients can tap to claim. Great for celebrations, tips, or community rewards.
